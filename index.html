About the Company

The RailEase project aims to modernize and enhance the country's railway infrastructure, catering to the growing demands of passengers and freight transportation. With a focus on efficiency, safety, and comfort, the project encompasses various initiatives, including the renovation of existing stations and the construction of new ones equipped with state-of-the-art facilities.
