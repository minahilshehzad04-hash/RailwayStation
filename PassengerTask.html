Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - RailEase eBooking

What is RailEase eBooking?+

RailEase eBooking is an online platform that allows you to book train tickets, check train schedules, and manage your bookings conveniently from your computer or mobile device.

How do I create an account on RailEase?+

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the RailEase homepage. Fill in your personal details, choose a username and password, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How can I check my booking status?+

To check your booking status, log in to your RailEase account and navigate to the "My Bookings" section. Here, you can view the status of all your bookings.

How do I book a train ticket?+

To book a train ticket, log in to your RailEase account, select your route and travel dates, choose your preferred train and class, and proceed to payment. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

Can I cancel or change my booking?+

Yes, you can cancel or change your booking by logging into your account and accessing the "My Bookings" section. Please note that cancellation and change fees may apply, depending on the fare rules.

How will I receive my ticket?+

Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive an e-ticket via email. You can also download and print your ticket from the "My Bookings" section of your RailEase account.

What payment methods are accepted?+

RailEase accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. Select your preferred payment method at checkout.

Is my payment information secure?+

Yes, RailEase uses advanced encryption technology to ensure that your payment information is secure and confidential.

How do I get a refund for a cancelled booking?+

Refunds for cancelled bookings are processed according to our cancellation policy. Once your cancellation request is approved, the refund amount will be credited to your original payment method within a few business days.
